Return Policy

At VIKJAN GROOT AGRICULTURAL PRODUCTS (OPC) PRIVATE LIMITED, we stand behind the quality of our products. If you're not completely satisfied, here's our return process:

1. Return Eligibility

We accept returns under these conditions:

  • Timeframe: Within 7 days of delivery
  • Condition: Unused, unopened, and in original packaging
  • Documentation: Original invoice must be included
Agricultural Products Note: Due to the perishable nature of agricultural goods, returns are only accepted for defective/damaged items or wrong shipments.

2. Non-Returnable Items

These products cannot be returned:

  • Opened perishable goods (seeds, fertilizers, etc.)
  • Products with tampered safety seals
  • Custom or bulk orders (unless defective)
  • Products damaged due to customer mishandling

3. Return Process

Follow these steps for hassle-free returns:

  1. Notify Us: Email returns@vikjangroot.com within 7 days
  2. Get Approval: We'll respond with a Return Authorization (RA) number within 24 hours
  3. Pack Securely: Include all original packaging and accessories
  4. Ship Back: Send to our warehouse address provided in the RA email

4. Return Shipping

Shipping responsibility depends on the reason for return:

  • Our Error: Free return shipping (wrong/damaged item)
  • Your Choice: Customer pays return shipping
  • Recommendation: Use trackable shipping method

5. Refund Processing

After we receive your return:

  • Inspection: 1-2 business days
  • Approval: You'll receive email confirmation
  • Refund Method: Original payment mode (except cash on delivery)
  • Timeline: 5-7 business days after approval
COD Orders: For cash on delivery returns, refunds will be processed via bank transfer (provide IFSC code and account details).

6. Damaged/Defective Products

Special instructions for agricultural products:

  • Take clear photos of damage before opening packaging
  • Preserve original labels and batch numbers
  • Do not discard damaged items until instructed
  • We may arrange pickup for heavy/bulk items
