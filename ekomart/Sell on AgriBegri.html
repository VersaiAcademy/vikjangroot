Frequently Asked Questions

What documents do I need to register as a seller?

You'll need:

  • PAN card
  • GSTIN certificate (if applicable)
  • Bank account details
  • Business registration documents (for companies)
  • Quality certifications (if any)
How much does it cost to sell on AgriBegri?

There are no upfront fees to join. We charge a commission only when you make a sale. Our standard commission rate is 15% of the sale price.

How and when will I receive payments?

Payments are processed weekly and transferred directly to your registered bank account. Payments are typically initiated every Monday for orders delivered in the previous week.

Can I sell both organic and conventional products?

Yes, you can sell both, but they must be clearly labeled and categorized correctly. Organic products require appropriate certification documentation.

What types of agricultural products can I sell?

You can sell fresh produce, grains, seeds, processed agricultural products, dairy, and related items. Prohibited items include live animals, illegal substances, and non-agricultural products.
